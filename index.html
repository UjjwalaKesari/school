The Khaitan School

About Us

Part of a legacy of industrialists who have been instrumental in nation building, the Khaitan family has since 1966, established and managed schools such as Khaitan Pre-School (Noida), Khaitan Public School (Ghaziabadh), BDM International School (Kolkata), Indus Valley World School (Kolkata), Sunny Preparatory School (Kolkata) besides others.

Established in 1995, The Khaitan School is a CBSE affiliated school located at Sector 40, Noida, which has been consistently ranked amongst the top schools of Noida. With a student strength of more than 3800 students, staff strength of 247 teachers / administrative staff and support staff in excess of 125 persons for child safety and hygiene (including CCTV operator, guards, lady guards, aiya’s and others), TKS is a thriving community of learners, parents, teachers and care takers. Our school bus service caters to most sectors of Noida, Greater Noida, Noida Extension and Delhi with the help of GPS and CCTV enabled buses. Parents are given a free bus tracking app that helps them track their bus in real-time and also notifies them when children board and de-board their respective buses.
